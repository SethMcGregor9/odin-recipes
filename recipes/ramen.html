Ramen

Description

Everything You Need For Homemade Ramen

Ingredients

Ramen noodles (our classic Maruchan package is all we need, sans the seasoning!) Garlic and ginger Broth (chicken or veg) Dried shiitake mushrooms Veggies like carrots or kale All your favorite toppings like some panko, egg, chili oil, etc.

Directions

Step 1:

Stir-Fry Aromatics: Garlic and ginger, what a delicious duo. This is where the flavor is, friends.

Step 2:

Make Your (Easy!) Broth: Add some chicken broth and dried shiitake mushrooms for some umami punch.

Step 3:

Add Noodles: Cook your noodles right in the broth with some scallions (more flavor, please!).

Step 4:

Add Veg: Thinly sliced kale, shredded carrots, whatever youâ€™d like! Cook until just tender.

Step 5:

Top It Off: Add some crunchy panko crumbs, a soft-boiled egg, chili oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, and/or soy sauce, whatever your heart desires.
